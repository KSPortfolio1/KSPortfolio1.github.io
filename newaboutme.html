Environmental Humanities

I decided to specialize in Environmental Humanities after taking a Caribbean literature class. This class empahsized the importance of thinking about how culture can change how environmental issues are addressed. I think it is very important to look to the people from a place when talking about the environmental issues occuring there to get clearer understanding of the issues at hand. We also talk about the importance of understanding how places will all experience the consequences of something like climate change differently.
